What WE OFFER Batteries:

A mechanic will test the viability of your current battery. There’s a possibility your lack of power has nothing to do with a dead battery. Other malfunctions can cause the same problems.

Battery Replacement:

While there are multiple battery cables under the hood, damage to even one of them will affect the smooth flow of current through the others, potentially causing expensive issues with the connected components themselves. If you suspect your battery cable might be damaged or dislodged, the best course of action is to verify that the cable is the culprit for your vehicle’s electrical issues.

Battery Cable:

From the battery terminals to the starter, charging system, lights and other power connections, the mechanic ensures that the cable ends are attached securely with a clean connection, that there is no corrosion on the battery near the terminals, and that the wires and terminals are protected by flexible coverings which have not significantly degraded over time.
